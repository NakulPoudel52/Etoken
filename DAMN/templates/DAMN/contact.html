About Us

About eToken eToken – Nepal’s first online token booking gateway is a comprehensive online token's booking solution. With eToken digital, customers can book many hospitals and banks token using their mobile phone and the Internet instantly. We have been in operation since 2020 and have been licensed by Goverment of Nepal I.T department as a Service Provider.

Our customers can use eToken web application for booking online token's of their respective Banks or Hospitals that saves their precious time of standing in a long queue for that purpose which can be done in the click of a button by using our services and also it's main purpose is to reduce the crowd gathering around such places following the recent crisis of the Covid-19 pandemic with which the whole world has been struggling to cope up.

Moving with the mission of leading the online token booking industry with a secured, integrated and comprehensive processing service, we carry a vision of Digital 2025 and hope to enable digital transactions for 30% of all Nepalese day to day life by 2025 A.D.
